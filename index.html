About Us

Agriteks has been servicing the blueberry industry since 2007. We are the exclusive Sales & Service for WECO Sorting & Automation, A&B Packing Equipment, and Lakewood Process Machinery in British Columbia. Our product lines include but are not limited to Blueberries, Tomatoes, Cranberries, Cherries, Potatoes, and many Vegetables. Our warehouse is located in Abbotsford, BC where we stock all spare parts for all of our manufactures. We also utilize our facilities to refurbish all WECO sorters during the off-season to ensure your sorter is ready for your busy packing season.
