About Us

Agriteks has been servicing the blueberry industry since 2007. We are the exclusive Sales & Service for WECO Sorting & Automation, A&B Packing Equipment, and Lakewood Process Machinery in British Columbia. Our product lines include but are not limited to Blueberries, Tomatoes, Cranberries, Cherries, Potatoes, and many Vegetables. We pride ourselves on having local service technicians to respond to all service calls 24/7. Our warehouse is located in Abbotsford, BC where we stock all spare parts for all of our manufactures. We also utilize our facilities to refurbish all WECO sorters during the off-season to ensure your sorter is ready for your busy packing season.

Our highly qualified service technicians all have automation backgrounds and strive to providing the best customer service for our leading state of the art technology and packing equipment.

WECO

WECO (Woodside Electronics Corporation), based in California, has been designing, manufacturing, and servicing electronic sorters for over thirty years and has thousands of units deployed worldwide. WECO serves several industries: tomato sorting on the harvester; the walnut industry; and several small fruits including, blueberries, cranberries and wine grapes.

Our Mission
Improve our customers' productivity and efficiency through automation. We strive to provide the highest quality, affordable products and services to our existing and future customers.

Our Vision
To be the leading provider of labor automation equipment to the industries in which we serve and to continue to find new industries and customers to whom we can provide value.

Guiding Principles
Put the customer first and ensure that our products and services provide our customers with maximum productivity and efficiency in their operations. We conduct our business with the highest level of integrity and honesty, both with our customers and within our company.

A&B

A&B Packing Equipment is a family owned U.S. company with a multi-agricultural background. We design and manufacture cutting-edge equipment for the 2lst Century that enables growers to meet the challenges of cleaning, grading, weighing and packaging fresh produce in the most innovative, gentle, accurate, and speed-efficient ways possible.

Our customer service follows the entire process from the initial floor plan layout and concept, to final completion and shipping—and A&B has trained field techs to provide on-site service. We are the “go-to” company for challenging packing needs and are known worldwide as the problem solvers for growers and packers alike.

A&B continues growing to meeting the demands of our customers—from North and South America to Europe, China and beyond.

Lakewood

Lakewood Process Machinery (LPM) has been a family-run business since 1964. For the past 45 years LPM has served a variety of industries, but with a primary focus on the agricultural and food processing industries, often providing innovative solutions to the challenges facing crop producers and processors. For the past three decades LPM has been a leader within the small fruit industry, offering a full line of packing and processing equipment to the berry industry.


On March 5, 2015, A&B Packing Equipment acquired the Lakewood Process Machinery product line. Both companies are extremely proud of the quality of their equipment, the quality of their personnel, and the quality of their service. “Customers can expect the same focus, the same high quality service and the same team effort that you have come to know” says A&B founder and CEO Bob Williamson.

If you have any questions regarding the recent acquisition please feel free to contact your sales representative at Lakewood or A&B’s president Fred Pieplow at 269-539- 4700.
